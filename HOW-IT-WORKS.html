Portal Automation

🤖 How we fetch data from government portals

You don't have to log in to 5 different websites. We do it for you — automatically and securely.

🔐
Step 1 — Credentials

You give us your portal password

Your Income Tax, GST portal and TRACES passwords are stored using AES-256-GCM military-grade encryption — the same standard used by banks. We generate a separate encryption key for every single client. Even our own engineers cannot read your password.

🌐
Step 2 — Browser Bot

Our bot opens the government website

We use a technology called Playwright — it's like having a robot that sits at a computer, opens a browser, types in the login details, and navigates the website exactly like a human would. No APIs needed. Works even when the government changes its website design.

📥
Step 3 — Data Download

Downloads all your data automatically

From the Income Tax portal: Form 26AS, AIS, ITR status, advance tax, outstanding demands.
From TRACES: Form 16/16A, TDS defaults, challan status, deduction details.
From GST portal: GSTR-2B, ITC ledger, cash ledger, notices.

🤖
Step 4 — AI Analysis

AI reads and analyses everything

Our AI (powered by Claude by Anthropic) reads all downloaded documents like a senior CA would. It finds mismatches between Form 26AS and salary slips, detects missing deductions, flags GST reconciliation errors, calculates your exact tax liability, and generates an audit risk score.

📊
Step 5 — Dashboard

You see a clean, actionable dashboard

Everything is presented in simple cards and tables. No more logging into 5 different government websites. See all your clients, their compliance status, deadlines, and AI-suggested actions — all in one place.

AI Assistant

🤖 Your personal tax expert — available 24/7

Ask anything in plain English. Get answers in seconds. No appointment needed.

Which tax regime saves me more money this year?
Based on your income of ₹18L and deductions worth ₹2.1L — Old Regime saves you ₹14,300 more. Your 80C, HRA and home loan interest make the old regime better for you.
Why does my Form 26AS show different TDS than my salary slip?
Your Form 26AS shows ₹48,000 TDS but your payslips show ₹52,000. The likely cause: your employer may have filed Q4 TDS late. Check with HR — the challan may be under TRACES processing.
What deductions am I missing? How much can I save?
You have unused 80C room of ₹62,000. Investing in ELSS or NPS can save ₹18,600 more in tax. Also, your parents' mediclaim (₹50,000 deduction) is not claimed.
Is my GST ITC claim correct? Any risk of notice?
3 invoices from Vendor XYZ (total ₹1.2L) appear in your books but not in GSTR-2B. Do not claim ITC on these — follow up with the vendor to file their GSTR-1 first.

Security

🔒 Bank-Level Security

Your clients' financial data is the most sensitive data there is. We treat it that way.

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

Every portal password is encrypted using the same standard banks use. Even if someone hacked our database, they would see only random characters — never real passwords.

🇮🇳

Data Stays in India

All data is stored on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) servers. Your clients' financial information never leaves Indian soil. Fully compliant with Indian data residency norms.

🔑

Zero-Knowledge Vault

Each client gets a unique encryption key derived from their own identity. Even our team cannot decrypt Client A's data using Client B's key. Complete isolation.

📜

Full Audit Trail

Every action — login, data fetch, computation, filing — is logged with timestamp, IP and user. Complete transparency for compliance and accountability.
